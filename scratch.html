Malware Mayhem’ is an innovative and educational game coded by the Scratch platform. This game is designed and manufactured to raise awareness of the cybersecurity issues looming in our co-existing world. This is executed by informing the players about Malware by listing and showcasing prevention strategies to further increase robust security. The players delve into the role of Cyberskull, an audacious digital saviour who must navigate various decently challenging parkour levels and collect antivirus protections to confront and defeat the Hacker Virus.
